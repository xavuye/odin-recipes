Lasagna is a italian dish

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made of stacked layers of pasta, cheese, and meat or vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Marinara sauce
  • Egg
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and Italian seasoning.
  4. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  5. Layer noodles, cheese mixture, mozzarella, and marinara sauce. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly.
  8. Let it cool before serving.
