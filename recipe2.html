Pizza is a classic Italian dish

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish made with a yeasted flatbread base, topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pepperoni or other toppings
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  5. Add pepperoni or other desired toppings.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle Italian seasoning.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Top with fresh basil if desired, slice, and serve.
