Burger is a classic American dish

Burgers are a popular dish made with ground beef patties, served in a bun with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and various toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup and mustard
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Form ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook patties for 3-4 minutes per side, adding cheese during the last minute.
  4. Toast burger buns lightly.
  5. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and desired condiments.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy.
